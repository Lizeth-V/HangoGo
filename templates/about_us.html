About Us

HangoGo is an AI-based hangout place recommendation web application. The app will aim to resolve the common problem amongst friend groups of having a difficult time choosing where to go.

How to use

Just type in your preferences, such as the type of place you're looking for or any specific requirements.

Hangogo will recommend places based on your preferences. You can simply accept or reject its suggestions.

When you find a place you like, simply click "Accept," and Hango will show you the location on the map to the left of your chat.
